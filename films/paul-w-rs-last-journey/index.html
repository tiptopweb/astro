PAUL W.R’S LAST JOURNEY

E DERNIER VOYAGE DE PAUL W.R.

DIRECTED BY: ROMAIN QUIROT

STARRING: HUGO BECKER, LYA OUSSADIT- LESSERT, JEAN RENO, PAUL HAMY, PHILIPPE KATERINE

In his first feature, director Romain Quirot creates a unique futuristic film, defying current French cinematic trends. Blade Runner meets Mad Max; 2001: A Space Odyssey meets Don’t Look Up! Flying cars, GPS spiders and a desolate backdrop with a soundtrack that carries the film to the next level.

In the post-apocalyptic wasteland, a red moon has appeared in the sky and is excessively exploited for its energy resources. Suddenly the red moon changes its course and threatens the earth’s existence. The only hope of saving earth and humanity is the enigmatic Paul W.R., the most talented astronaut of his generation. But a few hours before the start of the Great Mission, Paul disappears…

This high-concept jaunt through the galaxy features the best of what French cinema has to offer, acting that would feel at home in our everyday lives but coupled with direction that will inspire audiences with ambitious and beautifully-crafted worlds. Paul W.R.’s Last Journey is an ode to the fun of the 80s that feels fresh and original to the sci-fi genre. It’s excitement that’s not to be missed.
